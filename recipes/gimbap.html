Gimbap

Gimbap is a Korean roll made of seaweed (gim) and rice (bap). It contains contains colorful ingredients and can be made with all types of fillings.

I hope you enjoy making this!

Ingredients:
  • 5 sheets of seaweed paper
  • 4 cups of cooked rice
  • 1/2 pound of beef skirt steak, tenderloin, or ground beef
  • 1 large carrot, cut into matchsticks (about 1.5 cups)
  • 5 strips of yellow pickled radish
  • 8 to 10 ounces of spinach - blanched, rinsed in cold water, and strained
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 teaspoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon + 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1.5 teaspoons of kosher salt
  • 2.5 tablespoons of toasted sesame oil
  • vegetable oil
